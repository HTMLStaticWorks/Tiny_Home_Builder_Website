Our Story

Crafting Freedom Since 2018

We started in a small workshop with a big dream: to make intentional living accessible through high-quality architecture.

Small Spaces, Infinite Possibilities

Our mission is to help people simplify their lives without sacrificing the comforts of home.

TinyDream was born out of a desire to create something meaningful. We saw the housing crisis and the environmental impact of traditional building, and we decided to do something about it. Every tiny home we build is a step toward a more sustainable and free future.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in sustainable tiny home architecture.

Our Mission

Designing homes that inspire adventure and intentionality.

Our Core Values

What drives us every single day.
